A smile is a facial expression formed by flexing the muscles at the sides of the mouth. It is belived it takes about 80 muscles to smile. Some smiles include a contraction of the muscles at the comer of the eyes, an action known as a "Duchenne smile".Smiles performed without the eye contaction may be perceived as insincere.
